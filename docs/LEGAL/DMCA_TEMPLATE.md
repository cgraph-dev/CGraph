### GitHub

Send to: copyright@github.com

Or use GitHub's online form: https://support.github.com/contact/dmca-takedown

### GitLab

Send to: dmca@gitlab.com

### Bitbucket

Send to: copyright@atlassian.com

---

## Important Notes

1. **Only use for code released AFTER January 27, 2026**
   - Forks made before this date may contain MIT-licensed code, which is legal
   - Check the fork date in GitHub before sending

2. **Document everything**
   - Take screenshots of the infringing repository
   - Save a copy of the commit history
   - Note the date you discovered the infringement

3. **Consider contacting the person first**
   - Sometimes infringement is unintentional
   - A polite email may resolve the issue faster than DMCA

4. **Consult legal counsel for complex cases**
   - If the infringer is a company
   - If there's dispute about what was copied
   - If they contest the takedown

---

## Response Timeline

- **GitHub:** Typically responds within 1-2 business days
- **Repository disabled:** Usually within 24 hours of valid request
- **Counter-notice window:** 10-14 business days for the accused to respond
